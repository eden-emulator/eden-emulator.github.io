Privacy Policy

Eden can not and will not collect or use any of your data. For update checking (desktop only) and driver fetching, Eden will send anonymous requests to the GitHub API, so you may be subject to the GitHub privacy policies. Eden does not include any fingerprinting, user-agent, or identifying headers within these API requests.
